Find your new best friend today!


What to bring:

•We require a driver's license or any other form of photo ID for your adoption process.

•Adoption prices are $50 for cats and dogs, $100 for kittens and puppies.

•Our adoption fees include Spay/Neuter surgery, treatment for internal/external parasites, microchip, core vaccines,
Feline Leukemia testing for all cats, and heartworm disease testing for all dogs.

What to expect:

Adopting a pet is exciting, but it can also be stress-inducing for your new furry friend. Your new friend may be a bit nervous in their new home, please be patient with them as they take time adapt to their new environment. Their previous owner may not have trained them as you want them be trained. Be calm and patient with your pet throughout this process. If you have other animals in your home, please schedule a visit to the shelter for your new pet to meet them before taking them home.
