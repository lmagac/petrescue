Meet Bailey



Bailey

Female/Spayed
2 years old
Breed: Domestic shorthair/mixed
Declawed: No
Housetrained: Yes


Bailey is our newest arrival to the shelter. She was a stray that was found by one of our volunteers when she was left abandoned on a highway. Bailey is a cat who appreciates both play time and cuddle time. Bailey is shy around other animals, and unknown around children. She is potty trained and FIV negative.
