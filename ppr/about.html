About us and our cause


We are Penacony Pet Rescue, a non-profit organization determined to end the abundance of strays and homeless animals all across Florida. We pride ourselves on being a euthanasia-free shelter, and ensure all of our animals receive the best possible care we can offer.

Are you interested in our cause? Consider volunteering at our shelter or donating to help spread our cause and make this change a reality.

We are constantly in need of supplies for our shelter and we are grateful for every donation. If you would like to donate food or other pet supplies, please come visit the drop-off at our shelter.

Looking to relinquish an animal or report a stray? Contact us today.

Looking for a new furry friend? Come visit our shelter or look at our adoption page here.

With your help, we can ensure every cat and dog has a happy home to live in.
